Peanut Butter Bars

Description

Extremely sweet and taste peanut butter bars that are very similar in shape and in taste as the peanut butter cups. While they do have a slightly high calorie count, they're packed with proteins which makes them extremely handy when you need to supplement your diet with some protein.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine, melted
  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup peaunt butter
  • 1 1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the butter or margarine, graham cracker crumbs, confectioners' sugar, and 1 cup peanut butter until well blended. Press evenly into the bottom of an ungreased 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a metal bowl over simmering water, or in a microwave-safe bowl in the microwave, melt the chocolate chips with the 4 tablespoons peanut butter, stirring occasionally until smooth.
  3. Spread over the prepared crust. Refrigerate for at least one hour before cutting into squares.
